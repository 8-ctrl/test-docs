Overview

Zelthy Platform signifies a radical leap in application development, offering an innovative environment to foster seamless design, development, and integration of Healthcare applications. This platform is a testament to our unwavering commitment to simplifying complex development processes, empowering developers with a robust yet user-friendly toolset that eases the journey from conception to execution.

Our Core Principles​

Rethink App Development - Building an integrated framework that covers the entire spectrum of technologies that constitute a software application.
Open Software - Empowering technology teams to control and manage all aspects of their software products and applications. No proprietary lock-ins!
Natively available industry compliances: Making latest industry compliances natively available in the platform.
